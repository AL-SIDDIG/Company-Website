Management & Marketing Co. Ltd

Management and Marketing Co. Ltd (MMC) was established in 1992 to provide integrated logistics, courier, and administrative support services to both public and private sector organizations.

With decades of operational experience, MMC supports government entities, financial institutions, and corporate clients through secure, compliant, and technology-driven solutions designed to enhance efficiency and operational excellence.

Corporate Affiliation

Part of a Strong National Holding Group

MMC operates as a subsidiary of Saudi Postal Services Holding Company (SPS), a closed joint-stock company headquartered in Riyadh with a capital of 128,000,000 SAR.

Management & Marketing Co. Ltd (MMC)

Holding the franchise for Saudi Postal agency, managing postal rooms for banks, and providing logistics, parcel transportation, storage, and nationwide delivery services for government and commercial sectors.

Dar Al-Khat Logistics Services Co. (IPS)

Specializes in logistics, archiving, contracting, and e-commerce services, and holds the franchise for Saudi Postal international services.

Handba Ltd.

Operating local and international parcel transportation, holding the Saudi Postal agency franchise, and managing Al-Rayah and Al-Thaqbah agencies.

Al-Rowad Co.

Specializing in transportation and logistics services, supporting large-scale operational and distribution requirements.
